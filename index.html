Ms Sara Grace

Founder & Lead Art Instructor

Ms Sara Grace is a passionate artist and educator with over 8 years of experience teaching children the joy of creating art. She holds a Master's in Fine Arts from Jain University, Bangalore. She believes every child has an artist within, and her classes are designed to nurture confidence, focus, and self-expression. She is the perfect mentor to budding artists and creative souls. Her teaching is filled with patience and warmth. She gives individual attention to every student, imparting wisdom in the field of art.
